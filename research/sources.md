## Collection Methodology

### YouTube Transcripts
Collected via `yt-dlp` using auto-generated captions (VTT format, converted to plain text). Focused on videos from the last 12 months where possible. Channels selected are the expert's primary owned channels — not conference appearances or third-party interviews, to ensure consistent voice.

### LinkedIn Posts
LinkedIn does not provide a public API for post content. Manual collection was performed by reviewing each expert's recent public posts (accessible when logged in). Posts were selected for strategic density — prioritizing framework posts, opinion posts, and "hot take + reasoning" format over promotional content. Each post file includes the post text, approximate date, and engagement signals (likes/comments range) where observable.

### Filtering Criteria
- Posts that teach a *replicable principle*, not just share a win
- Posts with >500 likes (signal of resonance, not just reach)
- Posts published within the last 6 months where possible
- Variety of formats per author: text, carousel hook, list, story
