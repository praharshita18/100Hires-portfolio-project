# Newsletters, Books, and Additional Resources

Supplementary materials beyond YouTube and LinkedIn for each expert.  
These are primary or near-primary sources that deepen the research base.

## Dave Gerhardt — Exit Five Newsletter

**URL:** https://www.exitfive.com  
**Format:** Weekly email newsletter  
**Audience:** B2B marketers, CMOs, marketing leaders  
**Why it matters:** Exit Five newsletter is Dave's primary long-form content vehicle. LinkedIn posts are often excerpts or teasers; the newsletter is where full frameworks and case studies live.

**Recurring content types:**
- CMO interviews with revenue data
- "What's working in B2B marketing" roundups
- Playbook breakdowns (content strategy, LinkedIn, demand gen)

**Notable issues to reference:**
- "The B2B content flywheel" (companion to YouTube video CPlZXWFc7G8)
- "Why every B2B company should think like a media company"
- "How to build a LinkedIn following for B2B"

## Maja Voje — Go-to-Market Strategist Book

**Title:** *Go-to-Market Strategist: Everything You Need to Reach Product-Market Fit*  
**Published:** 2023  
**Publisher:** Self-published  
**Why it matters:** The most comprehensive GTM framework book written by a practitioner (not a VC or consultant). Maja's LinkedIn posts are distillations from this book; the book provides the full framework with examples.

**Key frameworks in the book:**
- The GTM Readiness Framework (used in her checklist posts)
- Category creation vs. category entry matrix
- Growth loop identification methodology
- The "10 Boxes" launch checklist

**LinkedIn:** Maja regularly shares excerpts and updates from the book's frameworks.
